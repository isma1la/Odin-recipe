Pizza Recipe

This is a great recipe when you dont qant to wait for the dough to rise. You just mix it and allow it to rest for 5 minutes and then it's ready to go! It yields a soft, chewy crust.

Pizza Crust Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Let the dough come to room temprature
  2. Prepare your surface with oil
  3. Shape the dough
  4. Add the topping
