Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a great recipe when you dont qant to wait for the dough to rise. You just mix it and allow it to rest for 5 minutes and then it's ready to go! It yields a soft, chewy crust. Quick, Simple and reliable.

Pizza dough Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps for the dough

  1. Combine the yeast, water and suger in a bowl then add salt olive oil to the flour
  2. Allow the dough to restfor a bit
  3. Shape the dough and spread the sauce on top
  4. Prepare the topping for later like chesse, veggies, fruits, or protein

Sauce Ingredient

  • Crushed tomatoes-1 can or fresh
  • Olive oil-2 to 3 cloves, minced
  • Garlic-2 to 3 cloves, minced
  • Dried oregano- 1 teaspoon
  • Dried basil-1 teaspoon
  • Sugar-1 teaspoon
  • Salt-to taste

Steps for the sauce

  1. Heat olive oil in medium saucepan iver medium heat
  2. Add minced garlic and saute for 1-2 minutes
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes oregano, basil and tomato paste
  4. Being to a simmer, then reduce the heat and let it cook for 15-20 minutes, stir occasionally
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. You can also add onion powder
  6. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly before spreading it on your pizza dough

Finally bake your pizza

  1. Add your topings you prepared before like chesse, vegetables and your desired protien
  2. Preheat your oven to 475 degree farenheit.
  3. Ounce its done preheating put your pizza inside the oven for 15 to 17 minutes

Enjoy your pizza
